What's an i-Show?
I had this idea for a show. Something new, different, and exactly what you've seen before. It's supposed to be like a late night talk show without the guests (because who is going to come to Michigan to be on some crappy internet show?) So I do some comedy bits for about twenty minutes a few times a week and see if I can get a cult following... AND THEN I TAKE OVER THE WORLD!!! (Insert evil laugh here.) I'm currently still in the development stages of the show. I'm trying to get enough material for a few weeks worth of shows and I still need a decent video camera. Plus, I'll need better internet hosting.
