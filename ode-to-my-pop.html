Ode To My Pop
Carbonated, caffeinated, bubbly goodness.

Get yours now.

In a can,

Or a bottle,

Or a really big bottle,

For those of you who need a lot.

You know who you are.

It's always on sale somewhere.

Hurry, you don't want to miss the special discount.

It's cheaper than gas,

Tastes better too!

Oh, how I love the Mountain Dew.
