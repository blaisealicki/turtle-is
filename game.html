Video Game Progress

Terrakon

The name of my game is Terrakon which is also the name of the world the game takes place on. I decided the name means "with Earth" even though I made it up.

Characters

Graf - Just a simple boy who's parents are killed by Garblins (Garblins are like goblins). Fights with a staff.

Marci - Decides to follow Graf on his quest, but refuses to say why. Fights with a knife.

Rianol - An Elf who is assigned by his people to protect Graf and Marsi from harm. Fights with bow and arrows.

Kyndle - An accidental creation of the Lizard Wizard. Fights with a sword.

RPG Toolkit

I am developing Terrakon with the RPG Toolkit Development System Version 2.2, available for free at http://www.rpgtoolkit.com.
