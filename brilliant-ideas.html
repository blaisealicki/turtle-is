Brilliant Ideas

Virus Cure For Cancer

Maybe it will work, maybe it can't, I don't know, I'm just putting it out there. A virus is specialized at killing cells. The protein coating of a virus tells the virus what kind of cells to go after and infect. If the coating could be changed so that a virus could attack a specific type of cancer cell then a patient could get an injection similar to a flu shot that would destroy only the cancer and leave the healthy cells.

2025 Update: there is ongoing work on this.

Online Grocery Shopping

It would have to be run locally, and be more like pizza delivery, but the orders would come in over the internet rather than by phone. The site would list the most commonly requested items to allow for easy ordering and the people taking the orders would then go to the store, pick up the items and deliver them for a small fee.

2025 Update: somehow this also became a thing.
